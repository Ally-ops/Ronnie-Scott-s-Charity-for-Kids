What we do for kids

We aim to ensure that jazz music education is avaiblable to all young people with a passion for rhythm and music, regardless of where they live, or their financial background.

Our fundraising provides access to music education for young people through free big band workshops, and sessions with established musicians in local schools.

One of our greatest achivements has been to distribute musical instruments to kids both at home and overseas through the generosity of musicians donating their instruments.
